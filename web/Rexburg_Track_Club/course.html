Programs

Track & Field

Summer 2019

A summer track & field program for boys and girls ages 8-14 hosted at Madison Jr. High School in Rexburg.Practices are held Mondays and Wednesdays 9:00-10:00am for six weeks starting June 3, 2019. Meets, most often Thursdays, begin the second week in June. A final meet concludes the season Saturday, July 6.

Former Ricks College coach Doug Stutz organizes local athletes/coaches to coach club members. The club is for beginners as well as experienced participants. Competition at meets is in the following ages groups: 8 & under, 9-10, 11-12, 13-14. Selected running, jumping, and throwing events offered. Club members receive a team t-shirt to wear as the team uniform at meets.

Cross-Country

Fall 2019

A fall cross-country running program for boys and girls grades 3-6 hosted at Smith Park in Rexburg. Practices are held Mondays and Wednesdays 5:15-6:00pm for eight weeks starting August 19, 2019. Meets, held most weeks, begin August 23. On weeks without a meet, an additional practice is held Fridays. A final meet concludes the season the second week in October.

Former Ricks College coach Doug Stutz coaches club members. The club is for beginners as well as experienced participants. Youth should look forward to running 2 miles or more at practices.

Competition at meets is in the following grade groups: 3rd & 4th and 5th & 6th. Most race distances are 1 mile. Club members receive* a tech t-shirt to wear as the team uniform at meets. The registration fee for those who do not participate in the 2019 summer track & field program is $100. For those who do, the fee is $80. *Those who participate in the 2019 summer program use the same club t-shirts for cross-country.
